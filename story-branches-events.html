Milestone 6 Branch: AI Rights Decision

At 500K KR (Milestone 6, index 5), gameplay pauses and presents a two-path decision. Both choices are playable, but they diverge heavily in economy and end-state risk.

Path A - Equal Rights (AI Transition)

2/3 Employees destroyed Recruiters liquidated GPU era enabled AI Auto-Organize at Lv.4
  • Immediately destroys 2/3 of Employees.
  • 1/3 of destroyed Employees are converted to IT Support drones (autonomous repair units for AI buildings).
  • Remaining Employees convert into AI at their current levels. Employee building type is locked.
  • After ~4s, all Recruiters are liquidated at 1/3 value → $. Recruiter building type is locked.
  • After another ~4s, GPU Era begins — GPU building unlocks.
  • Each AI now requires TFLOPS to operate (Lv.1=4, Lv.2=8, Lv.3=18, Lv.4=54). Under-provisioned AI produce reduced/zero output.
  • AI Auto-Organize is offered when any AI building reaches Lv.4.
  • Run remains winnable to final objective reviews.

Path B - Humans First (Robot Uprising)

Robot war mode Can't pass milestone 7 No IT Support 10× building damage Loss achievement path
  • Robot uprising begins — cannot progress past milestone 7.
  • IT Support disabled — healing is completely removed.
  • 10× building damage (150-950 per hit).
  • 100× KR drain — KRs are siphoned at 100× normal rate.
  • Zero KR production during the robot war.
  • Exponential strike escalation — attack frequency scales at 1.08^(elapsed seconds).
  • When KRs reach 0, all AI buildings self-destruct.
  • If no AI exists, a rogue AI spawns to lead the uprising.
  • Game over when all buildings are destroyed — AI takeover cinematic plays.
  • Losing this branch unlocks the Taken Over achievement.

What Triggers Which Event Systems?

Upgrade Unlock Cinematic

Triggered once when Objective Review 4 unlocks upgrades. Gameplay auto-pauses during playback and resumes after countdown.

Threat Systems (Review 6)

Technical Debt ships and competitor raiders activate after the 500K KR review. Competitors run KR-poaching attack loops on Leadership.

Competitor Intro Clip

Auto-plays once when the first competitor ship spawns, then marks intro as completed for that run/save state.

Random Event Engine (Review 7)

After 5M KR review, first random event fires in ~10-25s, then repeats every ~15-35s while the run remains active.

Phishing Catastrophe

On each random-event roll there is a 5% chance of a phishing instant game-over event (outside the 100-event pool), awarding the Phished! achievement.

VPN Enabled

When the first Network Hub is placed, particle flows between buildings stop. Unlocks the 🔒 VPN Enabled achievement.

AI Takeover Loss Clip

Triggered when robot-uprising path reaches zero remaining buildings and game over. This clip does not resume gameplay.
