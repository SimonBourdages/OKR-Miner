Engine Improvement Proposals — 10 visual upgrades to supercharge the game

#1Smooth Camera Pan & Zoom

Add mouse-wheel zoom and drag-to-pan so players can zoom into their factory network. Transforms a static viewport into a fluid exploration experience.

High PriorityMedium Complexity

#2Particle Trail System

Replace simple dot particles with smooth bezier-curve trails that fade over time, giving movement a fluid, energetic feel.

Medium PriorityMedium Complexity

#3Glow / Bloom Post-Processing

Draw bright elements to an offscreen canvas, apply a Gaussian blur, and composite back for a neon bloom effect on buildings and connections.

High PriorityHigh Complexity

#4Animated Connection Flow

Replace dot-based connections with flowing gradient energy streams that pulse rhythmically between nodes, conveying resource transfer visually.

Medium PriorityMedium Complexity

#5Building Placement Animation

When placing a building, animate it warping in — scale from 0→1 with a radial flash and slight overshoot bounce.

Low PriorityLow Complexity

#6Dynamic Background Stars

Add 3 parallax star layers drifting at different speeds, creating depth and immersion over the galaxy background.

Medium PriorityLow Complexity

#7Damage Flash Effect

When a building takes damage, briefly tint it red and flash white — instant visual feedback that communicates threat clearly.

Low PriorityLow Complexity

#8Resource Flow Visualization

Show tiny OKR/AC/EL icons flowing along MST connections from buildings toward the factory, making the economy feel alive.

High PriorityHigh Complexity

#9Minimap

A small corner minimap showing all buildings, connections, and threats at a glance — essential for large factory layouts.

Medium PriorityMedium Complexity

#1060fps Render Budget Monitor

Developer overlay showing frame time, draw calls, and node count — helps identify performance bottlenecks and maintain smooth gameplay.
