Description

OKR Miner drops you onto an uncharted asteroid where every decision shapes your destiny. Harvest rare minerals, research alien tech trees, and construct an engine powerful enough to punch through the cosmos. With 8 buildable structures, an AI companion that evolves alongside your strategy, and over 100 random events that ensure no two runs are alike — this is the engine-builder you've been waiting for.

Will you chase efficiency or gamble on the unknown? At Level 6 you'll face The Critical Choice — a fork in the road that permanently alters your path. Five hand-crafted visual themes transform every playthrough into a cinematic journey through the stars.

Features:
• 8 unique structures with deep upgrade paths
• AI companion with adaptive behaviour
• 100+ random events and encounters
• The Critical Choice at Level 6
• 5 stunning visual themes
• Endless replayability
